ABOUT THE COIN

Bitcoinz is a leading DeFi yield aggregator on BSC that has 2 products as part of the Bitcoinz ecosystem, namely Vaults (yield optimizer) and farmfolio. The yield optimizer implements the most optimal strategies at the lowest cost to maximize users' yields and Bitcoinz implements optimizations to route users swap trades so that users can find the best price and swap rates.
